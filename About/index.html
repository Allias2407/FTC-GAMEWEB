About this Website

Classic Game Recreation

This page introduces my final project: a modern take on Tetris and Pong. Tetris, with its timeless block-stacking challenge, and Pong, the pioneering paddle-based game, are classics that shaped gaming history. This project, built after 2 months of SEED learning in Fintech club, offers a nostalgic yet refreshed experience.
